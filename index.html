About Me

I'm Jhojo Indab a graphic & web designer, specialising in the fields of corporate identity design, re-press & digital printing and branding. Implement marketing promotions for businesses such as logos, websites, letterhead, siness cards, packaging and more... I had been in the graphic industry since 2004. My skills are very flexible in any type of design and multicultural environment.

Technologies and tools I use are Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, HTML5, CSS3, Sass, jQuery, Javascript, Sublime, Bracket, Balsamiq, Sketch, Git and Bootstrap.

Now, I spend most of my time honing my skills in Ruby on Rails and other technologies.
